Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play action-RPG available on Windows, Mac, IOS, Android, and PlayStation. The game takes place in a fantasy world called "Teyvat". Teyvat is composed of 7 nations namely: Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Sumeru, Fontain, Natlan, and Snezhnaya that are each ruled by 7 different archons (gods). These nations are tied to specific elements like cryo (ice), dendro (plants), pyro (fire), anemo(wind), etc. The story starts on a shore near Mondstadt. Where a traveler was put to sleep by an unknown god for hundreds of years and met a flying companion called Paimon. Now, they are off to travel to find the traveler's sibling through exploring the 7 nations mentioned. This game is also a gacha (gambling) game, where you can obtain your favorite characters and weapons through the gacha system.

Five reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • I can play with my friends because it is a multiplayer game with a maximum of 4 players per world.
  • You can join a player's world and ask for materials to upgrade your character!
  • It suits my gambling addiction without spending money, but your efforts to earn the game's currency to buy pulls.
  • I love the storyline! I think it's kind of unique.
  • I love the game's music. It relates to the nation's culture, and its genre is classical music.
